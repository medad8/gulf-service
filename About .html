GULF SERVICE STATION PROFILE

considering the services of gulf

Gulf Service station is located in Nairobi, Kenya It was established in the year 2005 operating with over 6 petroleum filling stations operating in different towns in Kenya; Mombasa, Lamu, Nairobi, Machakos, Thika and Gilgil. Nairobi being the main head office, and other offices in all the braches countrywide, we sell petroleum fuels; diesel, kerosene and engine lubricants. We also offer mechanical support like wheel alignment, service bay, car wash among others, we offer online booking for our products and services like vehicle servicing. Our products and services are fixed and offered at affordable prices in all the service stations countrywide and they do not sway due to demand
